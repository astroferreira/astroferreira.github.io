— Stability of Galaxy Morphometry

Here we investigated how non-parametric morphology is affected by cosmological dimming, resolution degradation, noise and angular depth. The main goal was to assess how our classificator performed with higher redshift images of nearby galaxies. (de Albernaz Ferreira & Ferrari, 2018)

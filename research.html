Research

Stability of non-parametric galaxy morphology in high redshifts

In this work we investigate how non-parametric morphology is affected by redshift: how the cosmological dimming, resolution degradation, noise and angular depth impcats in our measurements? Is it possible to classify galaxies even in bad scenarios like this? Where is the limit?
